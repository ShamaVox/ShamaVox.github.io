Advisory

Senior technical judgment, without the senior hire.

I work with founders and enterprise teams on the gap between a promising AI prototype and something you would put in front of your customers. Sometimes that's a two-week diagnosis. Sometimes it's twelve weeks of building alongside your engineers.

Engagement models

Four ways engagements usually start.

01 · 1–2 weeks

Architecture & reliability review

I assess an existing AI system or roadmap and return a written diagnosis: where it breaks under load, what's missing from evaluation, what the infrastructure will cost at ten times usage, and what to fix first. Fixed fee, fixed scope.

02 · 3–6 weeks

Design sprint

System design for a production agentic workflow — agent topology, verifier and repair loops, evaluation harness, failure handling, and cost model. Delivered as a specification your team can build against, with a reference implementation.

03 · 6–12 weeks

Build & ship

End-to-end delivery of a production system, working alongside your engineers or with a small vetted team. I own architecture, reliability sign-off, and the handoff — including the documentation your team needs to run it without me.

04 · Ongoing

Fractional CTO

Monthly retainer for technical direction, hiring architecture, vendor evaluation, architecture review, and board-facing translation — for teams that need senior judgment before they can justify a senior hire.

Where I help most

The problems that come to me.

  • A prototype that works in the lab and fails with real users
  • No way to measure whether AI output is correct before it ships
  • Cloud and inference costs scaling faster than revenue
  • Agents that fail confidently instead of failing safely
  • An AI roadmap that needs a technical reality check before a board meeting
  • An engineering org that needs to grow without losing delivery velocity
  • Sensitive or regulated data moving through an AI workflow

How I work

What you can expect.

  • I do the work myself. Contractors, when used, build under my architecture and sign-off
  • Written deliverables, not just conversations — you keep the artifacts
  • Fixed scope and fixed fee wherever the work allows it
  • IP assignment and data handling agreed in writing before anyone touches your systems
  • A handoff designed so your team can run the system without me
