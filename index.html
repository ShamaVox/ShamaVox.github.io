AI-native retail platform

Multi-agent enterprise document intelligence

Context
Unstructured vendor data was costing commerce businesses thousands of hours of manual overhead. "Usually right" isn't a standard a business can operate on.
Architecture
Multi-agent extraction converting unstructured retail documents into deterministic structured models via the Claude API, with real-time hallucination detection, custom confidence scoring, and stateful fallback guardrails — agents verify each other, escalate on low confidence, and fail into a known state rather than a plausible wrong one.
Result
96% data-extraction accuracy in production with live paying merchants. Engineered custom onboarding pathways around a platform certification bottleneck to safeguard the sales pipeline.
Stack
Claude API, multi-agent orchestration, confidence scoring, human-in-the-loop review tooling, commerce platform integration.
Nickelytics

Edge AI telemetry for autonomous assets

Context
Converting physical moving vehicles into measurable advertising inventory, which required validating precise edge attribution rather than loose estimation.
Architecture
Built real-time location tracking, edge attribution telemetry, computer vision at the edge, and the software foundations from absolute zero — plus the engineering organization to operate it.
Result
Scaled recurring revenue from $20K MRR to $3.5M ARR, owning product, delivery, infrastructure cost, and the org through the entire climb.
Stack
Edge computer vision, real-time geospatial tracking, attribution pipeline, cloud infrastructure engineered for sublinear cost scaling.
SignAI

Real-time multimodal generative translation

Context
Sign language interpretation is an edge processing latency problem disguised as a translation problem.
Architecture
Designed bidirectional real-time ASL–English interpretation using gesture and pose modeling, low-latency inference, and edge optimization on constrained hardware.
Result
Delivered a working real-time bidirectional prototype running on commodity hardware, and ran technical validation demos for Microsoft. Handed the platform off as a Deaf-led initiative — accessibility work belongs with the community it serves.
Stack
Computer vision, gesture and pose modeling, low-latency inference, bidirectional translation pipeline.
BrainEx · Side project

Confidential mental health support for students in India

Context
Students in India face significant mental health pressure and very little confidential support. The barrier is rarely willingness to seek help — it is the fear that seeking it will be visible to family, faculty, or peers.
Architecture
Designed a privacy-first data architecture where confidentiality was a system property rather than a policy document: strict access controls, data minimisation by default, and automated audit logging on every read of a sensitive record.
Result
A support workflow students could actually use, with the privacy guarantees enforced in the architecture rather than promised on a terms page.
